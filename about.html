Our Story

You've got stuff to do. We get it. You're practically epic. But you gotta have great coffee, and it needs to be fast and convenient. That's where we come in.

We created Coffee Shop where customers come inside so we can be a part of your story. It starts with the coffee. Our model is simple and elegant. We don't do much besides coffee, so we're really good at it. We don't have free wi-fi or places to plug in, so nobody sits around with their face stuck in a screen. They actually talk to each other.

Our Vision

Our simple approach also solves problems like waiting too long or paying too much. Our offerings and overhead are minimal, so we can pass the savings along to you, and do so in less time than most.
